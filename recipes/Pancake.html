Pancake

A fluffy and delicious sweet Pancake

Ingredients

  • 2 Spoons of all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 Spoon of melted butter
  • 1 Spoon of sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. Mix the egg light.
  2. Put the milk and sugar and mix
  3. Put the baking powder and mix light
  4. Put the mix in a not stick frying pan
  5. Serve with fresh fruits, syrup ou butter
